Verbal, Aptitude & Reasoning

This section focuses on developing critical problem-solving skills, logical reasoning abilities, numerical aptitude, and verbal communication. It is crucial for competitive exams, job assessments, and academic entrance tests.

The topics include number systems, percentages, time and work, blood relations, coding-decoding, syllogisms, reading comprehension, sentence correction, and more.

Ideal for students, job seekers, and professionals preparing for government exams, campus placements, or logical ability tests.

  • Improve problem-solving and analytical thinking
  • Master quantitative aptitude and arithmetic skills
  • Enhance verbal and non-verbal reasoning skills
  • Build confidence for competitive exam preparation

TARGET AUDIENCE:

  • Aspiring job seekers and freshers
  • Students preparing for CAT, GATE, GRE, SSC, Banking, etc.
  • Campus placement aspirants

PREREQUISITE:

  • Willingness to practice regularly
